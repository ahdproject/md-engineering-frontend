## The Five-Minute Overview

### What Is It?
A complete theme system that lets users choose between Black & White and Blue & Beige visual themes.

### How Does It Work?
1. User clicks theme button in navbar
2. Theme switches instantly
3. Choice is saved automatically
4. All pages respect the theme

### Why Is It Great?
